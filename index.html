Welcome to Puzzle Games

This is a website where you can play different puzzle games either with a friend or against various difficulity programs :)
Currently there are only 2 games (Click on them for instructions):

  • Tic-Tac-Toe is a game for two players who take turns marking the spaces in a three-by-three grid, one with Xs and the other with Os. A player wins when they mark all three spaces of a row, column, or diagonal of the grid. Click here to play

  • Connect Four is a game in which the players choose a color and then take turns dropping colored tokens into a six-row, seven-column vertically suspended grid. The pieces fall straight down, occupying the lowest available space within the column. The objective of the game is to be the first to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of four of one's own tokens. Click here to play


To Navigate to the game you want to play, just click on the corresponding icon in the navigation bar at the top of the page.
